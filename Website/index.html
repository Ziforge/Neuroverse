Welcome to the Empathy in VR Study

We are exploring how VR can foster empathy and understanding across diverse neurotypes. If you're neurotypical, neurodivergent, or have lived experience with severe mental illness, you're invited to join this immersive study.

  • Participate using a Meta Quest 3 headset
  • Short adaptive and static VR sessions
  • Completely anonymous, GDPR-compliant data
